# Bee buzzing sound effects

Place MP3 files here for the title screen and game-over screen:

- **buzz-title.mp3** – plays (and loops) on the title screen
- **buzz-gameover.mp3** – plays once when the game-over / restart modal appears

## Free sources (royalty-free)

- **Pixabay** – https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/bee/ (e.g. “Bee Buzzing”, “Bee flying loop”)
- **Freesound.org** – https://freesound.org/search/?q=bee+buzz (check license per file)
- **Free Sounds Library** – https://www.freesoundslibrary.com/bee-buzzing-sound/ (CC BY 4.0, attribution required)
- **Mixkit** – https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/bee/

Download a file, rename to `buzz-title.mp3` or `buzz-gameover.mp3`, and put it in this folder.
